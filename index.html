~ *Blink* Prototype ~

Meet Blink. Who is he and where does he come from? No one knows, not even him.
Blink has no memory of his past. But he does know one thing... time bends to his will. He sets out to find answers to the past. And he will. Because time is his.
Protip: Hold a time power and act, while your foes watch in horror as you slip outside the fabric of time.

Controls

W (Hold): Slow Time!

S (Hold): Stop Time!

D (Hold): Rewind Time!

A (Hold): Attack!

Spacebar: Jump

Left/Right Arrows: Movement

Warning: It is dangerous to go alone, there are 'features' everywhere.
Ok so they are bugs. But listen. It's gonna be sweet. You'll use your time powers to make it through obstacles and bosses.
